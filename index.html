Now All Online

The Meshan Saga is a free to distribute electronic fanzine dominantly supporting the gaming activities and interests of the members of the former History of the Imperium Working Group in New Zealand. It provides a wide range of material for Traveller fanatics. Although focussed on Traveller Meshan Sector, from issue 11 onwards it begins to incorporate material written by the membership for their other Traveller RPG interests. People from all around the world are invited to submit material.

If you'd like to submit something for publication in The Meshan Saga, simply contact FSpace Publications on their submission form or contact them for more information.

Note the currently available copies have some redaction through some older issues due to rights withdrawal from a former contributor.
